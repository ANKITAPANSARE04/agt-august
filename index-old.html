AIGUTECH ENTERPRISE ASSET MANAGEMENT

ITAM

Information Technology Asset Management (ITAM) is a strategic approach to managing an organization’s IT assets throughout their lifecycle.

devices

ITSM

Information Technology Service Management (ITSM) is a systematic approach to designing, delivering, managing, and improving IT services within an organization.

devices

MDM

Meter Data Management (MDM) is a system and process for collecting, validating, storing, and analyzing data from utility meters, such as electricity, water, and gas meters.

devices

RMS

A Records Management System (RMS) is a comprehensive solution designed to manage an organization's records throughout their lifecycle.

devices

NMS

A Network Monitoring System (NMS) is a crucial tool for maintaining the health and performance of an organization's IT infrastructure. It involves continuous surveillance of a computer network to detect and respond to any issues that may affect network performance, security, and availability.

devices

DTM

Data Threat Management (DTM) is a comprehensive approach to identifying, assessing, and mitigating threats to an organization's data. It encompasses various strategies, tools, and practices aimed at protecting data from unauthorized access, breaches, and other malicious activities.

devices

UAM

Utility Asset Management (UAM) for digital devices involves the systematic approach to managing the lifecycle of digital assets used by utility companies, such as smart meters, sensors, communication devices, and data management systems.
